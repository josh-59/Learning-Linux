Package Managers

Under Construction!

Package managers facilitate the installation, patching and removal of software; their importance is difficult to overstate. Modern package managers exist for every major open-source project, be it Debian, Python, Ruby, Rust or Arch, to name a few. They make it easy to acquire software to tackle the project at hand.
