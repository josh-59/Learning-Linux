About

It seems like there's a learning curve to modern Linux: It's relatively easy to install Ubuntu from a thumb drive and then get on the web, but to learn how to do... what it does... requires an enormous amount of learning. I'm not sure that the volume of information can be changed, but the process of learning it could be made easier.

This site is intended to provide accurate, practical, entertaining and down-to-earth explanations of concepts pervasive throughout Linux. Much documentation on the subject exists online; references have been provided.

Cheers!
